# Accommodation Strategy — United States

## Base Selection Logic

Pick bases by daily movement friction, not by map center:
- Walkability to transit
- Airport/rail transfer simplicity
- Night safety and return options
- True total cost with taxes/fees

## Stay Patterns by Trip Type

| Trip type | Better pattern |
|-----------|----------------|
| First-time city visit | One core base + focused neighborhood days |
| Park-heavy | Gateway town near park access window |
| Multi-city route | 2-3 strong bases instead of daily hops |
| Family trip | Fewer check-ins, larger room footprint, transit simplicity |

## Booking Filters That Matter

- Free cancellation window aligned with visa/permit uncertainty
- Late arrival check-in reliability
- Real transit access after 9 pm
- Elevator/accessibility fit where needed

## Hotel Fee Reality

Always check for:
- Resort/destination fees
- Parking fees
- Early check-in/late check-out charges
- Amenity charges not included in headline rate

## Neighborhood Rule

In high-cost cities, one-step-better transit access often beats one-step-cheaper nightly rate.
