# Washington, DC — Playbook

## Best Use Case

History, museums, policy landmarks, and walkable urban core with transit support.

## Core Advantage

Large concentration of major museums and monuments in compact planning radius.

## Route Strategy

- Group National Mall and museum-heavy days.
- Keep one evening monument loop (night lighting value is high).
- If adding Arlington/Alexandria, treat as separate half-day blocks.

## Booking and Timing

- Many Smithsonian options are free, but timed entry may apply for specific institutions/exhibits.
- Cherry blossom periods require crowd-aware booking and early starts.

## Stay Strategy

- Metro-accessible base is usually better than cheapest room outside core.
- If trip is short, prioritize time savings over nightly rate delta.

## Common Mistake

Trying to cover all museums in one visit. Focus by theme (air/space, art, history, policy).
