# Channel Playbooks

## Reddit (organic)

**When:** Technical, niche, or developer-focused products.

**Rules:**
- 90/10: 90% value posts, 10% product mentions — max
- Build karma in target subreddits BEFORE mentioning your product
- Never post "check out my product" — always frame as sharing experience or asking for feedback
- Best format: "I built X because Y problem, here's what I learned"

**Process:**
1. Identify 3–5 subreddits where your users are
2. Spend 1 week reading and commenting (no product)
3. Post a genuine "I built this" story with learnings
4. Engage every comment within 2 hours of posting
5. Link to product only if directly asked or in context

**Good post formula:**
- Hook: "I [did unusual thing] for [time period]"
- Story: What problem, what you tried, what worked
- Lesson: 3–5 concrete takeaways
- Soft CTA: "Built a tool for this: [link]" at the end

## Hacker News — Show HN

**When:** Developer tools, OSS, technical products.

**Best time:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9–10am US Eastern.

**Title formula:** `Show HN: [What it is] – [Key differentiator]`

**Process:**
1. Submit to Show HN (not Ask HN, not regular)
2. Reply to EVERY comment within the first 2 hours
3. Be humble, acknowledge limitations, explain tradeoffs
4. Have a working demo (not just a waitlist)

**What kills Show HN posts:**
- Paywall on landing page
- No working demo
- Defensive replies to criticism
- Vague value prop

## Product Hunt

**When:** Consumer apps, SaaS, design tools, productivity.

**Prep (2 weeks before):**
- Get a hunter with 1000+ followers to submit
- Prepare 5-image gallery + 60s demo video
- Line up 20+ people to upvote on launch day (email list, friends, communities)

**Day of launch:**
- Post at 12:01am Pacific
- Share in Slack groups, Discord, Twitter, email list
- Reply to every comment
- Don't ask explicitly for upvotes (against rules)

## Cold Email / LinkedIn

**When:** B2B, high-value customers, direct sales.

**Formula that works:**
```
Subject: [Specific pain point] at [Company name]

Hi [Name],

[1 sentence: I noticed you do X / you recently Y]

[1 sentence: We help companies like yours do Z result]

[1 sentence: Thought it might be relevant — worth a 15 min call?]

[Name]
```

**Rules:**
- Personalize the first line (takes 2 min, 3× reply rate)
- Max 3 sentences of pitch
- One CTA only
- Follow up once at day 3, once at day 7, then stop

**Tools:** Apollo.io (finding emails), Instantly.ai (sequencing), Hunter.io (verification)

## Twitter/X

**When:** Dev tools, B2B SaaS, thought leadership, open source.

**What works:**
- Build in public threads: "I'm building X. Week 1 update: [real numbers]"
- Share a counter-intuitive finding from your data
- Hot take + thread unpacking it
- "I analyzed 100 [things] so you don't have to"

**Format:**
- Hook tweet must stand alone (gets shown without thread)
- 3–7 tweets per thread sweet spot
- End with CTA: "If you found this useful, follow for more. I'm also building [product] that does this automatically"

**Post frequency for growth:** 1 thread/week + 3–5 reply comments to bigger accounts/day

## Email List (retention + referral)

Build from day one. Even 100 engaged subscribers beats 10,000 social followers.

**Capture:** Offer something useful (template, guide, tool) in exchange for email.

**Nurture sequence:**
- Day 0: Welcome + deliver promised value
- Day 2: Teach one useful thing (no pitch)
- Day 5: Share your story / why you built this
- Day 10: Soft pitch + social proof
- Ongoing: 1 email/week with genuine value

**Tools:** Resend (transactional), Beehiiv (newsletter), ConvertKit (automation)
