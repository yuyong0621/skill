# Pacific Northwest — Playbook

## Best Use Case

City + nature blend with forests, coastline, coffee/food culture, and road-trip optionality.

## Main Anchors

- Seattle metro and nearby mountain access
- Portland + Columbia Gorge extensions
- Olympic and Cascade side trips

## Planning Notes

- Shoulder seasons deliver value but can include wet and variable weather.
- Trail and mountain access can shift quickly with snowpack and closures.
- Reserve iconic park lodging and ferries early in peak periods.

## Pace Recommendation

Use one city base and one nature base for 7-10 day trips.
