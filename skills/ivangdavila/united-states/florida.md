# Florida — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Warm-weather trips, beaches, theme parks, and mixed family/adult travel.

## Region Profiles

| Zone | Best for | Caution |
|------|----------|---------|
| South Florida (Miami/Fort Lauderdale) | Beaches, nightlife, Latin food culture | Parking and nightlife price spikes |
| Central Florida (Orlando) | Theme parks and family planning | Queue strategy and heat management |
| Gulf Coast | Slower beach rhythm | Car dependency in many segments |
| Florida Keys | Scenic drives and water activities | Limited lodging inventory and weather sensitivity |

## Seasonal Rule

Hurricane season runs June-November; flexible bookings and storm contingency matter.

## Family Execution Notes

- Theme-park days require reservation/queue strategy, hydration blocks, and rest windows.
- Avoid stacking full-day parks without recovery days.
