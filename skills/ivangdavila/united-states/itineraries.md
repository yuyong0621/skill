# Itineraries — United States

## 7 Days: One-City + Nature Hybrid

### Option A: New York + Hudson Valley
- Days 1-4: NYC neighborhoods, museums, food, one evening skyline window
- Days 5-6: Day trips or one overnight outside core city
- Day 7: Buffer + departure

### Option B: Los Angeles + San Diego
- Days 1-3: LA by zones (do not cross city repeatedly)
- Days 4-5: Pacific coast/Orange County stop
- Days 6-7: San Diego + return

## 10 Days: Two-Base Region Trip

### Option A: California Loop (Flight + Car)
- Base 1: San Francisco Bay (3-4 days)
- Transfer: Pacific Coast or short flight
- Base 2: Los Angeles/San Diego (4-5 days)
- Keep one flex day for weather/traffic

### Option B: Northeast Corridor (Rail-leaning)
- Base 1: NYC (4 days)
- Base 2: Washington, DC (3 days)
- Optional: Philadelphia/Boston add-on with restraint

## 14 Days: Parks + Cities

### Southwest Parks + Vegas/Phoenix
- 2 city buffer days + 8-9 park/road days + 2 flex days
- Secure permits/timed entry before finalizing lodging

## 21 Days: Three-Region Maximum

- Region 1 (cities): 6-7 days
- Region 2 (parks/coast): 7 days
- Region 3 (culture/food/south): 6-7 days
- Do not exceed 3 major regions unless trip is very transfer-tolerant

## Itinerary Quality Checks

- Every 3-4 active days include one lighter day
- Every transfer day has at least one backup option
- Last day before international departure stays in departure city
