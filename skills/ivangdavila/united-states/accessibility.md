# Accessibility — United States

## Planning Priorities

- Confirm step-free transit options in each city segment.
- Verify hotel accessibility details directly, not only listing tags.
- Avoid back-to-back transfer days when mobility support is needed.

## Airports and Security

- TSA Cares offers guidance for travelers needing additional screening support.
- For complex assistance needs, pre-notify airline and airport support channels.

## Urban Strategy

- Choose one highly connected base over multiple difficult transfers.
- Prefer attractions with clear accessibility documentation and timed entry.
- Keep buffer time between activities.

## Nature Strategy

- Select parks/trails with published accessible facilities.
- Weather can affect practical accessibility more than static maps suggest.
