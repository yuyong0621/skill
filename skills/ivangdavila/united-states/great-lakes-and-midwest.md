# Great Lakes and Midwest — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Architecture, music/sports cities, food depth, and strong value compared with coastal hubs.

## Key Anchors

- Chicago for architecture, museums, lakefront rhythm
- Great Lakes summer loops (Michigan/Wisconsin segments)
- Twin Cities and regional nature add-ons

## Season Notes

- Summer and early fall are strong for lake and city balance.
- Winter can be severe; build weather contingency into rail/flight movement.

## Routing Rule

Cluster urban cultural days with one slower regional excursion day.
