# California — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Coastline + cities + parks in one state, with high variety and high transfer complexity.

## Regional Split

| Subregion | Core strengths | Typical base |
|-----------|----------------|--------------|
| Bay Area + North Coast | Food, architecture, redwoods access | San Francisco/Oakland side |
| Central Coast | Scenic drives, wine, beach towns | Monterey/Santa Barbara bands |
| Los Angeles Basin | Entertainment, food diversity, museums | Westside/Hollywood/Koreatown by purpose |
| San Diego Corridor | Beaches, family-friendly pace, border culture | Downtown/La Jolla/Mission Valley |

## Planning Rules

- LA movement is zone-based; avoid multi-zone daily zig-zag.
- Pacific Coast segments need slower pacing than map distance suggests.
- Yosemite/other park add-ons require permit and season checks.

## Good 10-Day Pattern

- 4 days Bay Area + nearby excursions
- 1 transfer/coastal day
- 4 days LA or San Diego split
- 1 flex/weather buffer
