# open-items

## Purpose
Use open items to track unresolved risks, observations, and follow-up tasks.

## Suggested statuses
- `todo`: action needed
- `watch`: observe for now
- `idea`: future enhancement
- `done`: completed follow-up

## Suggested history fields
- `ts`
- `from`
- `to`
- `note`

## Principle
Open items are for meaningful follow-up only. Do not duplicate routine log entries here.
