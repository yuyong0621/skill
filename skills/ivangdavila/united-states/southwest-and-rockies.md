# Southwest and Rockies — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Iconic parks, geology, hiking, scenic drives, and stargazing-heavy routes.

## High-Value Clusters

- Utah/Arizona parks loop
- Colorado Rockies + Denver front range
- New Mexico culture + desert landscapes

## Critical Constraints

- Extreme summer heat in desert sections
- Altitude adaptation in Rockies
- Timed-entry/permit systems at specific parks and corridors
- Limited late-night services in remote towns

## Route Design Rules

- 2-4 nights per base in park corridors
- Start hikes early; protect midday from heat
- Keep a full weather backup day every 4-5 active days

## Common Failure Mode

Underestimating driving and overestimating same-day multi-park feasibility.
