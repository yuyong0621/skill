# Competitive Intelligence Report Templates

## Weekly Brief Template

```markdown
# Weekly Competitive Intelligence Brief
Week of: [DATE]

## Executive Summary
[2-3 sentence overview of most significant competitor moves]

## Key Changes Detected
### Pricing
- [Competitor]: [Change description]

### Product/Features  
- [Competitor]: [New feature or update]

### Content/Messaging
- [Competitor]: [Blog posts, messaging shifts]

### Social Activity
- [Competitor]: [Notable social media activity]

## Implications & Recommended Actions
1. [Action item based on competitor move]
2. [Opportunity identified]

## Next Week Watch List
- [What to monitor closely]
```

## Monthly Deep Dive Template

```markdown
# Monthly Competitive Landscape Report
Period: [MONTH YEAR]

## Market Position Summary
| Competitor | Pricing Tier | Key Differentiator | Recent Momentum |
|------------|-------------|-------------------|-----------------|

## Detailed Analysis
### [Competitor Name]
- **Changes this month**: [count]
- **Pricing**: [current pricing, any changes]
- **Product**: [new features, deprecations]
- **Content strategy**: [blog frequency, topics]
- **Social presence**: [follower growth, engagement]

## Trend Analysis
[Patterns across competitors - are they all moving in the same direction?]

## Strategic Recommendations
[Actionable insights based on the data]
```
