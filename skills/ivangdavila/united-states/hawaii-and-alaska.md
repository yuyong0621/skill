# Hawaii and Alaska — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Longer-stay nature-first trips where one destination can carry the whole itinerary.

## Hawaii Strategy

- Fewer islands with deeper stay usually beats island-hopping speed runs.
- Rental car needs vary by island and stay area.
- Book popular activities early in peak periods.

## Alaska Strategy

- Summer and shoulder windows dominate accessibility.
- Distances are large and logistics are cost-sensitive.
- Wildlife/cruise/rail segments need earlier commitment.

## Shared Rule

Treat both as standalone trips unless duration and budget are clearly high enough.
