# Leadership & Strategy Playbook — Full Reference

Prepared: March 2026

This reference is educational and not legal, financial, or regulatory advice.

## Domains Covered

1. Strategic planning and vision setting
2. Decision-making under uncertainty
3. Change management
4. Crisis leadership
5. Stakeholder management
6. Corporate governance
7. Succession planning
8. Long-term thinking

## 1) Strategic Planning and Vision Setting

High-standard strategy includes:

- explicit choices and trade-offs
- coherent reinforcing activities
- strategy-to-execution linkage through metrics and owners
- regular assumption refresh and reallocation discipline

Recommended artifacts:

- one-page strategy template
- strategy map and scorecard/OKR cascade
- scenario and signpost pack

## 2) Decision-Making Under Uncertainty

Best practices:

- assumption-first decision records
- premortems for major initiatives
- scenario options and decision tripwires
- reversibility-based decision speed
- post-decision learning reviews

## 3) Change Management

Execution model:

- Kotter for organization-level momentum
- ADKAR for individual adoption
- barrier removal and capability building
- reinforcement via systems and incentives

## 4) Crisis Leadership

Core principles:

- prepared continuity playbooks
- clear incident roles and decision rights
- transparent and empathetic communication
- continuous learning via AAR and action closure

## 5) Stakeholder Management

Operating discipline:

- map influence/interest and materiality
- engage early and proportionately
- maintain commitments tracker and feedback loops
- measure trust and responsiveness outcomes

## 6) Corporate Governance

Governance quality indicators:

- clear accountability architecture
- active risk and controls oversight
- board agenda balance: strategy, talent, risk, controls
- evidence-backed governance disclosures and follow-through

## 7) Succession Planning

Always-on model:

- critical role coverage and emergency plans
- readiness horizon tracking
- internal bench development plus external calibration
- transition playbooks and onboarding support

## 8) Long-Term Thinking

Long-term value creation requires:

- portfolio-level capital and talent reallocation
- multi-year incentive and governance alignment
- resilience and capability metrics
- second-order effect analysis in major decisions

## Practical Operating System

Cadence:

- annual strategy + allocation reset
- quarterly strategic refresh and risk updates
- monthly operating and change portfolio review
- weekly leadership decision/escalation sync

Core tools:

- one-page strategy
- decision record and assumption tracker
- premortem worksheet
- change checklist (Kotter + ADKAR)
- crisis communication checklist (CERC-informed)
- after-action review template
- stakeholder map
- board governance calendar
- succession dashboard

## Maturity Checks

- Can leaders explain strategy and trade-offs clearly?
- Do budgets and leadership time match strategic priorities?
- Are assumptions explicit and reviewed on schedule?
- Is change adoption measured by behavior and outcomes?
- Are crisis routines rehearsed and learnings closed?
- Are succession pipelines current and actionable?
- Does governance consistently prioritize long-term value?
