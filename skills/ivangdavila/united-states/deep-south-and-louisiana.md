# Deep South and Louisiana — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Music heritage, distinct food traditions, and slower-pace culture routes.

## Core Anchors

- New Orleans for music, food, architecture, and festivals
- Nashville/Memphis for music-focused routes
- Charleston/Savannah style routes for history + coastal rhythm

## Execution Notes

- Heat and humidity shape daytime pacing in warmer months.
- Festival calendars can transform pricing and availability.
- Nightlife-heavy plans need explicit late-night transport safety.

## Food and Culture Rule

Reserve headline restaurants, but protect budget with neighborhood food strategy.
