# Nightlife — United States

## Nightlife City Types

| Type | Examples | Pattern |
|------|----------|---------|
| Mega-metro | NYC, LA, Chicago | Distributed neighborhoods, late options, transit planning required |
| Entertainment hubs | Las Vegas, Miami, New Orleans | Venue-driven nights, dynamic pricing, heavy weekend surge |
| College/younger hubs | Austin, Nashville zones | Live music density, peak queues on weekends |

## Execution Rules

- Prebook headline clubs/shows when relevant.
- Use licensed rides at close-out hours.
- Keep one low-intensity morning after heavy nightlife days.

## Safety Basics

- Monitor drink handling and personal belongings.
- Keep return transport pinned before going out.
- Prefer neighborhood hops over cross-city late-night jumps.
