# Family Travel — United States

## Family-First Planning Framework

1. Pick fewer bases with longer stays.
2. Balance high-energy days with recovery blocks.
3. Choose lodging with kitchen/laundry when trip is longer than one week.

## Destination Fit by Family Style

| Family profile | Better fit |
|----------------|------------|
| Theme parks + easy logistics | Orlando/Central Florida |
| Museum + city learning | Washington, DC + NYC |
| Nature and wildlife | National park loops with moderate driving |
| Beach + downtime | Gulf Coast, Southern California, Hawaii |

## Daily Rhythm Rule

- Morning: top priority attraction
- Midday: indoor/rest/meal break
- Late afternoon/evening: lighter activity

## Risk Reduction

- Heat and hydration planning in summer
- Reservation planning for top attractions
- Transit simplicity over maximum attraction count
